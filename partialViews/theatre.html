The Theatre


Brief history

Built in 1766 as a stopping point on a circuit of northern theatres The Georgian Theatre experienced a chequered history for much of its first 200 years or so – flipping from vibrant theatre to becoming a home for the local Salvation Army; falling into disuse before finding a new purpose as a sweet factory – final dereliction and threatened demolition in the 1960’s / 1970’s…

And now

Almost 25 years, many hundreds of performances and a major refurbishment later; we now have the venue brought into the 21st century – with an increase in capacity, comfort and operating hours – offering Stockton Borough, Teesside and the north east of England an amazing facility to nurture live music.

Capacity

The venue itself holds up to 300 people standing at a time. The Georgian Theatre can be home to a varity of acts such as live music, comedy.film and theatre. It is fun for all of the family and everybody is welcome.

Hiring the Georgian Theatre

Whether you’re an independent promoter or an artist looking to stage your own events; or looking for a really cool space to stage a party or other celebration – we can make your event work for you. Our venue is set up to host amazing performances or to celebrate your special occasion; our bar and kitchen can provide you with a range of beers, ales, gins and cocktails – alongside a well-constructed food offer, ranging from a simple buffet to something more special.
